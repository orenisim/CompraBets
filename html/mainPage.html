    About The Game

    The game will be active bettween 20.11.2022 - 18.12.2022 for the FIFA world cup 2022 games. The final score of the Group stage games will be determined after 90 min, and the final score of the Knockout stage games will be determined after 120 min. Each user has to guess which team will win the tournament until 20.11 at 12pm, and the guesses of the game result will be done until 12pm at the specific game day. Every morning the score will be calculated according to the previous games day , thus the table will be updated every day until 12pm.

    The Score Calculation

    • Guessing the winning team of the tournament = 10 points.
    • Guessing the correct winner or tie = 1 point.
    • Guessing the exact score (super score) = 3 points.
